Project Flowchart Description

This project is for the 2nd year, first semester, but I showed the flowchart because we lost our source code in C++. This is all about the cart system, and the flow of it includes a "display menu" where you can choose the items to "add to cart." In the add to cart section, there are 3 cases of products that you can add to your cart. You can also "view cart" to see the items you purchased, and you can "delete cart" if you added too many products. There's also a "checkout" option, so after viewing your purchases, you can check out immediately. Then you "exit" and go to the notes to see your receipt. However, if you just "exit" without checking out first, it will error out, and you won't be able to see your purchases because you didn't choose "checkout" before "exit." This is the flow of our flowchart and code.
